We presented our work consisting in the identification of areas of less constraints (environmental and social) for the settlement of floating offshore wind farms via a spatial optimization approach.

View the online conference proceedings of merIGéo 2023: https://www.merigeo.fr/Edition-2023/Actes-du-colloque.

This work is the result of a collaboration as part of the multidisciplinary research project APPEAL (Agence Nationale de la Recherche - France Energies Marines)
